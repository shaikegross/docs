---
title: 'Adding a Customer'
---

####  Customers can be added in one of three ways:
	 1. Imported via CSV file
	 2. Salesforce or Hubspot integration
	 3. Adding manually - as described below

>  To initiate the customer creation process, start by selecting "Customer" on the main menu and then click the "+" button. <br/>
>  Next, add all required information, including Customer Name, Address, and Contact details. <br/>
>   Once completed, simply click the "save" button to finalize the process.
