
-   Go to the Plans and Pricing page
-   Hover over the plan you wish to assign,  
    then click on the three dots menu and select **“Assign contract”**
-   Define the starting date according to the customer's contractual terms and click “Finish”.
    
It is also possible to assign a contract directly by using the **"save and assign as a contract"** from within the plan editor by using the 'Save' *dropdown menu*.
