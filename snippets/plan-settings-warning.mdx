<Note>
  ### Important Plan Settings Considerations

  Before finalizing your plan configuration, please review these critical settings:

  <AccordionGroup>
    <Accordion title="Billing Interval Settings" icon="calendar">
      * Billing intervals cannot be changed after the plan is activated
      * Ensure alignment with your customer's billing cycle preferences
      * Available options: Monthly, Quarterly, Semi-Annual, Annual
      * Mid-period changes will be prorated automatically
    </Accordion>

    <Accordion title="Plan Duration Configuration" icon="clock">
      * Set an appropriate end date that aligns with your contract terms
      * Consider implications for commitment periods and minimum terms
      * Future-dated plans will remain inactive until start date
      * Grace periods should be factored into the overall duration
    </Accordion>

    <Accordion title="Auto-Renewal Settings" icon="rotate">
      * Enable auto-renewal only for plans with consistent terms
      * Auto-renewed plans inherit all settings from the original plan
      * Pricing changes require manual intervention before renewal
      * Configure renewal notifications to allow time for modifications
    </Accordion>

    <Accordion title="Common Pitfalls" icon="triangle-exclamation">
      * Verify all pricing tiers before activation
      * Double-check billing frequency matches contract terms
      * Ensure proper configuration of base amounts and commitments
      * Review all settings before assigning to customers
    </Accordion>
  </AccordionGroup>

  <Warning>
    Once a plan is activated and invoices are generated, certain settings cannot be modified. 
    Always review thoroughly before activation.
  </Warning>
</Note>
